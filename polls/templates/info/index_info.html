About Us

Following the meeting of the Monitoring Structure of the Alert and Contingency Situation, due to the the Covid 19 pandemic, the government decided to set up multidisciplinary teams to visit all the homes of portuguese elderly, in order to verify compliance with safety rules and safety plans pandemic-related contingencies.

The monitoring structure monitors and produces regular information on the evolution of situations in force on the continent's territory, integrating representatives of the National Guard (GNR),Public Security Police (PSP), Foreigners and Borders Service (and the National Emergency and Civil Protection Authority (ANEPC), as well as Secretaries of State in the areas economic affairs, foreign affairs, the Presidency of the Council of Ministers, the Justice, Public Administration, Education, Labour, Health, the Environment, the Infrastructure and Housing and Agriculture
